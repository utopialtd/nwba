North West Buy Local App Rules

  • The North West Country Buy Local App is an app designed to encourage local residents in the North West Country area to buy and shop locally in return for a chance to enter a monthly draw to win prizes.
  • Prizes will be advertised on the North West Country website each month.
  • Participants must spend $10 or more in any participating store, then scan that business's QR code in the app in order to gain their points.
  • One scan per transaction of $10 or more is required to enter.
  • There is a maximum of one scan per business, per day – unless otherwise stated in specific promotions.
  • Participants/entrants must be 17 years or over to claim prizes or use the app.
  • Employees of participating businesses are permitted to enter the monthly prize draws, provided they meet the requirements for scanning the QR code at businesses they are not employed at.
  • In registering a profile for the prize draw, app users are consenting to being acknowledged as the monthly winners in any North West Country advertising.

Prize Draw:

  • Google and Apple are in no way affiliated with any contest/prize draw.
  • Prizes will be drawn on the first business day of every month.
  • Winners will be notified by phone.
  • Winners will be advertised on the North West Country website and Facebook page.
  • If winners cannot be contacted or do not respond to claim their prize within 24hours of being notified, North West Country Inc. reserves the right to redraw the prize.
  • Any contact information NWC receives will be treated confidentially, and will only be used for contacting winners of the monthly prize draws.
